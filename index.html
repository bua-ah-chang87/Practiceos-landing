How it works

From fact-find to
SOA draft in three steps.

01
Enter client data

Paste raw meeting notes, upload a fact-find PDF, or fill in fields manually. PracticeOS extracts and structures the data automatically.

02
Generate your SOA

One click drafts all six SOA sections simultaneously — Executive Summary through to Compliance Notes — with Australian regulatory standards built in.

03
Review, check, export

Run an automated compliance check against AFSL obligations and Best Interests Duty. Edit any section, save versions, then export to Word or PDF.
